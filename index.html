About

Hello World, I'm Mark!

Hi everyone! My name is Mark, and I'm a junior attending Wesleyan University. The summer of 2025, I decided I wanted to dabble with web-development and so here is my second little website! I hope that as time goes, I learn more about web-development, especially on creating apps and interactive webpages!

Currently, I am majoring in Computer Science and Philosophy. While, my experience for both remains rather lacking, I am confident that with enough practice and time, my growth will show. Whether that be creating webs, designing games, pondering the ethics of AI, or uncovering various viewpoints of life, I'm always ready to learn more!

Beyond academics, I enjoy learning new languages (currently studying Japanese), lifting weights, and plucking my little ukulele. Also, if it wasn't apparent, I'm fond of Capybaras!

The future is uncertain; however, we have the potential to alter it. Whether or not such outcome proves to be worthwhile, we ought to hold, grasp, and shape our lives with as much vigor and passion as one possibly can.
